Case Study

Enhancing MedGrocer's User Experience and Brand Consistency

Evaluated website, planned brand identity, created user-centered design maps and wireframes, and made recommendations for improvements.

Recognition: Kada’s Choice Award by KadaKareer’s Executive Team

Timeline: May - June 2023

Services: User Interface, User Experience, Quality Assurance, Usability Testing

Team: 3 UI/UX Designers

Technologies: Figma, Google Sheet

Desktop Prototype Mobile Prototype

Background

MedGrocer, a rapidly growing "medicine-as-a-service" company, offers digitally-enabled online pharmacy services, corporate clinics, and patient programs, empowering consumers to conveniently, cost-effectively, and intelligently access their medicines.

Challenges

  • Unite the look and feel of MedGrocer's platforms under their signature branding.
  • Create a consistent user experience across different versions of the website.
  • Improve design and implementation consistency.
